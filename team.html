Meet the Team

Meet the therapist

Anthony (Tony) Else has been a physical therapist since 1991, working in a variety of settings including homecare, outpatient/impatient rehab, and acute hospital settings before opening his private practice in 2002.

Tony obtained his education from the University of Langston with a Bachelors of Science in Physical Therapy. He then attended the Ola Grimsby Institute receiving his manual certification, and is now in pursuit of his fellowship in manual therapy with aspirations of obtaining his PhD.

He takes a personalized approach with his patients, and provides a quality learning and training environment for physical therapy students.

Office Administrator

Wendy Else has been serving g the community of Brevard since 1995. She has work experience across a variety of public health care facilities, but has specialized in the field of physical therapy since 2001. Wendy decided to join forces with Tony in 2010, helping him grow OverComers into the thriving business that it is today.

Wendy obtained her education from the Unviersity of Central Florida with a Bachelors of Public Administration.

Meet the Therapist Assistant

Rodd Cassell is origninally from Columbus, OH but has lived here in Florida with his wife for the past five years. He served our country in the United States Army for ten years before attending Eastern Florida State College obtaining his Physical Therapy Assistant degree.

In his spare time he enjoys staying active and playing volleyball. he has a passion for Physical Therapy and tries to make his work fun and enjoyable for not only himself but his patients as well.
